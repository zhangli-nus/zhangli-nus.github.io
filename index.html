Working Experience

Nov 2017~now, Senior Algorithm Engineer & Team Lead. Mainly focus on POI and delivery service in new retail(新零售线下门店和地址服务). We have served for 手淘收货地址服务, 飞猪基础景点酒店生产和服务, 天猫新零售门店智能入驻管理和口碑饿了么入淘上的服务. Our data scale is in billion level. There are around 5 billion delivery addresses, more than 100 million raw poi and 500 million user base. The fundamental of our service is based on an automatic big data fusion system to produce standard poi. We use the produced standard data to 1)correct delivery address in taobao app so that delivery can be assigned efficiently; 2)provide smart trip recommendatation in fliggy to help user automatically generate trip plan; 3)help market operalist to help store merchant efficently digitize the offline store in tmall; 4)help hotel merchant and ticket operationalist to generate high quality contents. In details, our services inclucdes 智能地址检索, poi归一, 异常地址检测, 智能行程, 智能文案生成. Various techniques have been proposed, such as multi-relation metric learning(WE NAMED IT AND WILL MAKE IT ACADEMIC AVAILABLE), text generation based on image-text correlation(WE FIRSTLY DEFINE THIS PROBLEM AND PATENTED OUR SOLUTION), smart trip recommendation, fine grained image classification and pretrain-nlp/cv models. You can view taobao app, fliggy and new retail to see our products. For this work, i was awarded the best staff in Alibaba.
