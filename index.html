Short Bio

I obtained my Ph.D degree from School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS), where I was supervised by by Prof Terence Sim. Before joining Alibaba, I was leading a research group in Mozat for two years, a local internet firm in Singapore. Up to date, I was granted five patents with over ten publications. I'm severing as the local organizer in Alibaba for CCKS and an adjunct supervisor in ShenZhen university.
My research aims at exploiting deep learning on multi-modality data including text, image and video for face recognition, fashion analysis and geographic service.
